Core Rules (Nun Saakin & Tanween)

Izhaar إظهار

Meaning: To make clear.

Recite the Nun clearly without extra nasalization when followed by Throat Letters (ء هـ ع ح غ خ).

Idghaam إدغام

Meaning: To merge.

Merging the Nun into the following letter. Occurs with letters in: ي ر م ل و ن

Ikhfaa إخفاء

Meaning: To hide.

A hidden sound between Izhaar and Idghaam with Ghunnah (nasalization). Occurs with 15 remaining letters.

Iqlab إقلاب

Meaning: To convert.

Changing the Nun/Tanween into a small 'Meem' sound when followed by the letter ب.
